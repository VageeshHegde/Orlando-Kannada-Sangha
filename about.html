About Orlando Kannada Sangha

Our Story

Orlando Kannada Sangha or OKS was formally registered as an organization in the year 2012. Although we have had a Kannada group and have been organizing activities since more than 20 years, we had not formally registered as an organization until 2012.

Orlando Kannada Sangha is an association for people that hail from the Karnataka state of India, and those who can speak the Kannada language. Its primary goal is to provide a platform for Kannada speaking people to meet, socialize and promote their cultural and social heritage to the next generation of Kannadiga's living in US.

Our Mission

To preserve and promote Kannada culture, language, and traditions in the Orlando area while fostering a strong community bond among Kannadigas.

Our Vision

To be the leading cultural organization that connects and empowers the Kannada community in Central Florida through cultural activities, education, and community service.

History of Orlando Kannada Sangha

The journey of Orlando Kannada Sangha began informally in the early 1990s when Kannadigas in Orlando started gathering for cultural activities. The organization was formally registered in 2012, marking a significant milestone in our community's development.
